Thesis Title: Enhancing Winter Wheat Crop Yield Predictions: A Data-Driven, Incremental, and Integrative Approach with Machine Learning. Solved multi-modality data problem by designing and implementing innovative ML model architectures to improve winter wheat crop yield predictions.
