About Me

I have completed my PhD from CNRS and University of Bordeaux in Dec 2022. I primarly worked in the area of frustrated magnetism, looking for exotic phases in real materials. I developed numerical methods such as Monte Carlo to generate phase diagrams for many-body systems. I thoroughly enjoy programming and math and have been exploring the field of data science, machine learning and Natural Language Processing(NLP). I enjoy solving problems in novel ways with state-of-the-art methods.
