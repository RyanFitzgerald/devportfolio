Projects

Macgyver

Have you ever thrown out a seemingly worthless odd or end only to realize it's true "value" just a bit too late? With Macgyver, you can easily make that experience a thing of the past! Macgyver allows you to search for lifehacks that use common household items to see how the item you are about to throw away could be reused.

Hobbeet

Hobbeet is an app that allows you to socialize while still keeping your routine. Whether you are scoping out a study buddy or a salsa partner, you can look up places or activities in your area and see who is doing what where to plan your daily adventure.
