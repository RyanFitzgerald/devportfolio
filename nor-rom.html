Sonar Integration - Drone Modular Smart Pallet

Python, ROS, Sonar Sensor, I2C Bus, Raspberry Pi

The project revolves around aggregating data from several different sensors, and fusing this information together using the Robot Operating System (ROS) to guide a roomba successfully through obstacles. I was responsible for developing software to aquire range data from three sonars that utilized the I2C bus on a Raspberry Pi. In addition, I integrated the sonars with ROS by developing publisher and subscriber nodes and launch files. See experience section to learn more about my contributions.

PR3 Hotel Management System

C#, .NET, Window Forms, MSTest, JSON, Firebase Database

This application allows the front desk employee to perform necessary operations such as check-in/check-out of a guest, view a report of available/occupied rooms, and look up the name of a particular guest along with their room number. In terms of limitations, the hotel’s capacity is only 40 rooms, and it accepts guests who are 18 years or older. All the required information will be stored in a local and in Google's FireBase Realtime Database. See experience section to learn about my contributions.
